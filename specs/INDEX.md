## v1.4+ roadmap (fidelity polish + capability expansion)

The v1.0–v1.3 set made the boat recognizable end-to-end (hull + deck + interior + propulsion + colors + DS variant). These specs drain the deferral backlog accumulated across specs 007–016: smoother geometry, real glass, detailed hardware/fittings, wider export + CLI surface, and the two long-standing quality fixes (FCStd determinism, parametric robustness). Ordered by value; reorder freely.

- [x] 018 — hull-surface-curvature-v2 — full track — **RE-SCOPED 2026-06-02 after a FreeCAD spike empirically killed the B-spline premise** (`Ruled=False` AdditiveLoft overshoots the beam ≥12% vs the 1% bar across uniform/Chebyshev/stem/amidships spacing; a raw `Part.BSplineSurface` skin would violate constitution III).
